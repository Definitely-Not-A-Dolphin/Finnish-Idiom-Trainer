The preesens is the Finnish equivalent of the present simple. It is used to described things that are being done at the moment.

To conjugate a verb in the Preesens, do these steps:
1. Take the verb (e.g. close -> sulkea),
2. Remove the last letter: sulkea -> sulke,
3. Apply KPT-rule (only for minä, sinä, me and te), sulke -> sulje,
4. Add the correct ending (depends on the pronoun), sulje -> suljemme

The correct endings for all the pronouns are:
Minä ~n,
Sinä ~t,
Hän / Se / tämä / tuo ~[last vowel],
Me ~mme, 
Te ~tte,
He / nämä / nuo ~(vat/vät),

Keep in mind that sometimes exceptions will apply!
